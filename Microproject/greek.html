Greek Cuisine

Overview

Greek cuisine is known for its healthy, flavorful and fresh ingredients. It is based on the Mediterranean diet and uses olive oil, herbs, and spices. Greek cuisine has a rich history dating back thousands of years and has been influenced by other cultures throughout history. Some popular Greek dishes include moussaka, tzatziki, and souvlaki.

Popular Dishes

Moussaka

Moussaka is a traditional Greek dish made with layers of eggplant, ground meat, and a creamy bechamel sauce. It is baked in the oven and served hot.

Tzatziki

Tzatziki is a Greek dip made from yogurt, cucumber, garlic, and dill. It is a popular appetizer and is often served with pita bread, vegetables, or as a topping for gyros.

Souvlaki

Souvlaki is a popular Greek street food consisting of small pieces of marinated meat (usually pork or chicken) that are grilled on a skewer and served with pita bread and various toppings such as tzatziki, onions, and tomatoes.
