Austrian Cuisine

Overview

Austrian cuisine is characterized by its hearty and comforting dishes, often incorporating ingredients like meat, potatoes, and seasonal vegetables. From the famous Wiener Schnitzel to rich, creamy soups, Austrian food is both filling and flavorful.

Popular Dishes

Wiener Schnitzel

The Wiener Schnitzel is a thin, breaded, and fried veal cutlet that is a staple of Austrian cuisine. It is typically served with a side of potatoes and lingonberry jam.

Goulash

Goulash is a traditional Austrian stew made with beef, vegetables, and a variety of spices. It is a hearty and comforting dish that is perfect for a cold winter day.
